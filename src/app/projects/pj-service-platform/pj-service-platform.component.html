Interaction Paradigm

One key to this project was creating clean flows, testing them out, and then making them cleaner. Vehice oriented info management and data access could be a input-intensive process that requires the work of multiple roles: the technicians taking hands-on practice and the engineers running trouble shooting. I worked on this flow until it was as smooth as it could be, prototyped iteratively to define a new interaction paradigm for the product’s overall main task.

  • Create Sharable Design solutions and Assets

Impacts & Results

Gained significant support and positive feedback from our business stakeholders. We were able to roll out the system step by step within the timeline.

Transforming technology using one unifying solutions that break down traditional silos. It is one platform that easily secured and manage track all the end points.

Laid out the foundation for design solutions and have style spects documented to be easily adopted by new designers, and even engineers were able to apply solutions without designers' presense.

By building out the component library and asset site, we were able to

  • Create modularized and consistent design solutions across platforms,
  • Help multiple projects simutaneously to deliver end to end solutions under limited headcount.
  • Save engineers from time-consuming styling tasks while guarding the aesthetics of design.



My Contribution

  • Leading the project to identify requirements by conducting early stage stakeholder interviews and market research.
  • Seting up design system and sketch style library for upcoming designers to follow as a reference point.
  • Established interaction paradigm serving as the templates of internal systems.
