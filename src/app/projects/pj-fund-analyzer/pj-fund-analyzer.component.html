What is Fund Analyzer?

The Fund Analyzer automatically applies a fund’s applicable fees and commonly available discounts, The Fund Analyzer helps you sort through and compare more than 30,000 mutual funds, exchange-traded funds, exchange-traded notes and money market funds, The Fund Analyzer does the math for you and shows how fees and expenses impact the value of your fund over time.

Design Task

In the project, our team were responsible for redesigning the Fund Analyzer with a more intuitive and user friendly UI. In this practice, we tried to adopt Microsoft’s newly released fluent design for its simplicity and mordern look and feel.

Search Result View

Under such artistic approach, we minimize the use of decorative line and color background, and put primary focus on spacing and treatment on elements interactivity to make information stands out.

Comparison View

During the design process, stakeholders with in-depth understanding on the product keep emphasizing that past gain doesn't project the future profit. So any number revenue or profit was just a forecast.

As only the calculation of Fee is accurate, we populate it at the top of the section. adding 2 decimal points to make the display looks more accurate the visualization grows toward bottom, so its fit user’s mental modal as the cost, negative and substraction. indented display to show visual hierarchy, and nesting relationship of fees right align numbers for easy commparison, and emphasize the key number, adding visual decoration toward bar chart try fit all information in one screen so you don’t lose the info desity and allow easy comparison, scanning.

Visualization Design Challenge

At the initial stage of design, we tried several fancy visualizations. however those proposals all faces potential technical challenges. Less is more, keep the design simple and straightforward, minimizing text to ink ratio.

Challenge: can’t be too technically challenging to accomplish, considering the technical constraint as part of the project. No much animation, js control on elements level.

Displaying Number Format

Right align is rarely seen in web design, but such treatment is clear for number display. Make labels right aligned for more association across the same row. UI treatment need to be consistent. medium black is for clickable, grey being the label

put percentage on one side to maintain a consistency in design patern As green and red can represent opposite market performance across culture, the arrow up and down is necessary as a key indicator. Rank the general summary at the top of chart to remain interaction consistency

In this redesign, we put clickable elements together All price related calculation are gathered together, as future value is estimated based on past growth rate. It is not a trustful source, but helpful source on making future predictions

Color coding practice

In Average Annual Return Section, according to lessons learned from previous practices, legend color assigned to each fund is somewhat associated with their [Load Type] . This helps users associate the funds easily and build up mental model. According to the previous user surveys, most users would like to put different classes of same fund when making comparison. So such solution would serve most senarios.

If different funds with the same [Load Type] are selected, shades and variant of this load color would be used.
