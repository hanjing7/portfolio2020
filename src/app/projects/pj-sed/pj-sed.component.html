General Intro

BYTON is building the smart cockpit for the future of digitalized generation with a connected experience. Its featued 48-inch long dashboard screen display sets the tone for the cabin experience.

Since the touchpad on the steering wheel is positioned far away off the big screen, designing for this digital mobile living space has to follow the logic to make it convenient and safe experience.
